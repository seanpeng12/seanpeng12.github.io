About me

I graduated with a degree in Information Management. During my undergraduate studies, I focused on both front-end and back-end software development, as well as database design. I also gained experience with AWS cloud technology and Linux system administration. For my capstone project, I developed a “Travel Analysis and Planning Website,” which included a keyword filtering function to help users reduce time spent searching for reviews. I was responsible for the full-stack development and integration of the website. This project not only enhanced my technical abilities but also helped me realize the importance of team communication.

  In my senior year, I interned at E.SUN Bank as a software developer. I primarily used the JAVA Spring framework to develop back-end APIs and integrated them with the front-end using JavaScript. This experience provided me with in-depth knowledge of fintech applications, particularly in electronic payment systems, and it strengthened my understanding of the importance of stability and security in software development.

  After entering the graduate program in Computer Science, I further explored computer architecture and operating system design, focusing on enhancing my knowledge of software-hardware integration. I also took courses in Digital Integrated Circuit (DIC) Design and Advanced Digital Integrated Circuit (ADIC) Design, where I learned the entire IC design flow, from Verilog descriptions to synthesis, automated placement and routing, verification, and post-layout simulation. I used fast-SPICE tools to simulate an All-Digital Phase-Locked Loop (ADPLL), which helped me develop practical skills in the field of digital IC design and computer-aided design(CAD).

  My research focuses on privacy-preserving de-identification face recognition systems. The goal is to maintain recognition accuracy while employing noise addition or spatial transformation techniques to achieve feature de-identification, thus enhancing robustness against reconstruction attacks. This research not only improved my problem-solving abilities but also deepened my interest in data privacy protection.

  Through both my academic research and internship experience, I have come to understand the critical importance of team collaboration and continuous learning. I am always eager to explore new technologies and look forward to applying my knowledge and expertise to future challenges, where I can continue to grow and contribute my professional skills.
